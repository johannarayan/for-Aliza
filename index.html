2025  ·  Something I've been meaning to say

Dear Aliza,

There is something I have quietly carried for a long time — a feeling I kept folding up and tucking away, telling myself the right moment would come. It never quite did. And now high school is ending, and the world is about to pull us in different directions. So before it does, I am choosing to finally be honest — not to complicate your life, but because some things deserve to be said at least once.

I always imagined a quiet moment — just the two of us — where I could look into your eyes, and you could look into mine, and without a single word, you would just know. Know that every time I saw you, something in me got a little warmer. Know that your smile had a way of making ordinary days feel like they were worth remembering.

"I like you."  —  No, that isn't quite right.
I love you, Aliza. And I think I always have.

I don't know everything about your life right now, and I would never assume. But if there is someone already in your heart — I understand. Truly. I appreciate that, and I respect it completely. I'm not writing this to pressure you or to ask you to choose anything. I just needed you to know that these feelings exist, quietly and honestly, on my end.

What I do find myself wondering — gently, with no weight attached — is whether there might be even the smallest chance for us. Not today, not necessarily tomorrow. Just… someday. If the timing ever feels right, if you ever find yourself curious about what this could be — I'll be here. No rush. No expectations. Just an open door.

I won't pretend I was everything I could have been. I didn't always show up the way I should have. But what I know without any doubt is that what I feel for you has always been quiet, steady, and completely real.

I hope your board exams go beautifully. I hope you walk in calm and walk out knowing you gave everything you had. I hope every dream you've held quietly finally gets the space to breathe and bloom.

Keep smiling that smile. Keep growing into whoever you are becoming. And wherever life takes you — I hope it is somewhere magnificent, full of light, somewhere that feels exactly like you.

This is not a goodbye. This is just me, finally being honest — and leaving the door open, for as long as it takes. 🌙

With all the love I never quite said out loud,
Someone who is still here, still hoping. 🌙
